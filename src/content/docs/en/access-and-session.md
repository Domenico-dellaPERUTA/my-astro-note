# Access and Session

The application manages two types of permissions to ensure the security of your data.


## Guest Mode
By default, every time you open the application, you will be in **guest mode**. In this mode you can:
- Read all existing notes.
- Consult this guide.
- Search through your notes.

In guest mode it is **not possible** to create, modify or delete notes or files.
