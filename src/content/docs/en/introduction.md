# Welcome

This application has been designed to help you organize your ideas in a hierarchical and quick way.

## Main Features
- **Hierarchical Notes**: Organize notes into folders and subfolders.
- **Quick Access**: Search and find your notes instantly.
- **Security**: Protect your data with secure login.
